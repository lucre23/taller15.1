All about them Fun facts Best songs Newsletter
The band was formed on the British television show The X Factor in 2010.
Their debut album "Up all night" became the first album by a UK group to enter at #1 on the US Billboard.
They quickly gained international recognition; their fans call themselves Directioners. Thanks to them, they have over 50 million records sold worldwide.
Their single "Best Song Ever" accumulated over 10 million views within 24 hours.
In August 2015, they announced an indefinite hiatus after completing their fifth studio album, "Made in the A.M"
The band was involved in various charitable endeavors, including supporting the Teenage Cancer Trust and raising funds for Comic Relief.
One Direction significantly influenced pop culture, leading to the creation of numerous fan sites, merchandise, and even a concert film, One Direction: This Is Us.
After the band’s hiatus, each member found success in their solo careers.
